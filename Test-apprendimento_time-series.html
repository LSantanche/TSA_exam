Predictive maintenance

Course final project

TIME SERIES ANALYSIS

10/02/2022

Predictive maintenance analysis are designed to help determine the condition of in-service equipment in order to estimate when maintenance should be performed. This approach promises cost savings over routine or time-based preventive maintenance, because tasks are performed only when warranted.
